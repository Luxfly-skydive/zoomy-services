Frequently Asked Questions

Can I cancel my monthly membership anytime?
Yes — cancel any time with no fees. Your membership stays active until the end of your billing period.
Is the free trial really no credit card required?
Absolutely. Sign up, attend for 7 days, then decide. We'll ask for payment details only when you choose to continue.
Can I freeze my membership if I travel?
Monthly and Annual members can freeze their membership for up to 60 days per year for no charge.
Are towels and lockers included?
Yes — towel service and full locker room with showers are included with all memberships. Drop-in visitors can rent a locker for $2.
How do I book classes?
Download the Zenflow app or use the website. Monthly+ members can book 7 days in advance; drop-ins can book 48 hours ahead.
What if a class is full?
Join the waitlist in the app. You'll be automatically added if a spot opens — usually it does.
