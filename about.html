Our Story

Why Zoomy exists.

Most agencies sell hours. We sell finished work. That difference is everything — pricing, pacing, scope, the way we communicate.

Traditional studios stretch a website into three months because they bill by the week. We use modern AI tooling — Claude, Gemini, and a tight stack we know cold — to do the same work in days. The thinking is the same. The polish is the same. The deadline is shorter and the price is fixed before we start.

No account managers. No timesheets. The person who scopes your project is the person building it. Direct line, daily updates, no handoffs.

Remote-first. Clients in NYC, Paris, Athens, Montreal, and a few we still can't pin on a map.

Values

How we work.

01 / NO TEMPLATES

Every site is hand-coded.

No WordPress, no Webflow, no off-the-shelf themes. Every site is written from scratch — no bloat, no plugin sprawl, no monthly platform fees. Faster sites, no lock-in, full control.

02 / NO DRAFTS

Everything we ship is final.

No "concept previews," no placeholder copy, no to-be-filled-in-later. When we hand a project over, it goes live the same day. Real content, real images, real working links.

03 / NO LOCK-IN

You own everything.

The code, the domain, the analytics, the worker that powers your chatbot — all yours, on your accounts. If you ever want to walk away from us, you take all of it with you. No one is held hostage to a vendor.
