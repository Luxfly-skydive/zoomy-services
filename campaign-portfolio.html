What we deliver

Three things that make campaigns work.

Every Meta campaign needs the same foundation — clear creative, clean tracking, and a properly structured setup. We build all three.

01
Deliverable 01
Campaign
Files

We build your campaign from the ground up — all the structured files you need to launch or hand to a media buyer. Audience configurations, ad copy, UTM structure, creative briefs, and a complete setup document. Everything is documented and ready to use.

  • Campaign objective and adset structure defined
  • Audience configurations built and documented
  • Ad copy written for each placement and format
  • UTM parameter structure configured
  • Creative brief for each ad format included
  • Full setup document — ready to upload or hand off to your team
02
Deliverable 02
Pixel &
Tracking

Ads are only as good as the data feeding them. We install, configure, and verify your Meta Pixel and conversion events so the platform optimises against real signals. Whether it's a fresh setup or an existing install that needs auditing, we make sure everything is working before a single dollar is spent.

  • Meta Pixel installed and verified across all pages
  • Conversion events configured — purchase, lead, add-to-cart, and more
  • Event deduplication checked
  • UTM and GA4 cross-checked against pixel data
  • Test events confirmed firing before go-live
  • Fix report provided with all changes documented

Deliverable 03
Ad
Creatives

Short animated ads built for Meta — feed and story formats, delivered as MP4 and ready to upload directly to Ads Manager. We animate from your brief. No footage needed. Available as standalone production or as part of a full campaign setup package.

  • Feed (4:5), Story (9:16), and Square (1:1) formats included
  • Animated from your product, message, and audience brief
  • Delivered as MP4 — ready to upload directly to Ads Manager
  • Copy and CTA written to match each placement
  • Revisions included
  • AI-generated video available on request

Whether you're starting fresh or inheriting a broken setup, we get the data flowing correctly before money is spent optimising against bad signals.
